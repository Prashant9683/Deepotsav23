Deepotsav 2023
On 8th November, 2023
Time: 4:30 - 7:30 PM
Venue: Convocation Hall

With great enthusiasm, we, the students of Amritapuri Campus, are delighted to unveil our idea for an extraordinary Diwali celebration on November 8th, 2023. Diwali, known as the Festival of Lights, stands as an eminent cultural festivity. Our intent is to craft an event that not only reverse time-honored traditions but also kindles a sense of unity and joy within our college community.

Cultural Event

A joyfull night filled with all cultural events including a DJ night, so join us NOW

An enthralling lineup of cultural performances will grace the stage, encompassing traditional dances, melodious music, and captivating theatrical acts. We encourage students to participate and unveil their hidden talents. So register by clicking the below button and show your talents such as Dance, Singing, Acts etc.

Rangoli Designing & decorating

Show your design talents and join us in decorating ASE to its peak beauty.

Our initiative entails active student engagement in adorning the ASE campus with unreserved fervor.
